Our Story

From humble beginnings to creative excellence

How Pure Prints Media Started

Pure Prints Media was founded in 2017 by a group of passionate creative professionals who shared a common vision: to deliver exceptional visual storytelling services that help businesses and individuals stand out in an increasingly digital world.

What began as a small photography studio has evolved into a full-service creative agency offering photography, graphic design, videography, and comprehensive branding solutions. Our growth has been driven by our commitment to excellence, innovation, and genuine client partnerships.

Our Journey

2017: Founded Pure Prints Media with a focus on professional photography services for events and portraits.

2018: Expanded services to include graphic design and branding, recognizing the need for comprehensive creative solutions.

2019: Added professional videography and video production services to our portfolio, allowing us to serve clients in the growing digital video market.

2020: Adapted and thrived during the pandemic by developing innovative digital solutions and remote collaboration capabilities.

2021-2025: Established ourselves as a premier creative agency in Kenya, completing over 500 projects for more than 150 satisfied clients across various industries.

Today, Pure Prints Media continues to push creative boundaries, embrace new technologies, and deliver work that makes a real impact for our clients. We're proud of our journey and excited about the future.
